To delete a booking, remove its event in Google Calendar - the slot reopens on the site automatically. To stop taking a day or time, use the Booking times tab (blocked dates / weekly hours).
Live booking backend
Live slot booking on the public site runs through a free Google Apps Script that writes to your Google Calendar. Deploy apps-script/Code.gs from the repo (see README), then paste its web app URL (ends in /exec) below and save. Leave blank to show call/text contact options instead.
